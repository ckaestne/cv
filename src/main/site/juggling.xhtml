Juggling in Pittsburgh

Where to juggle in Pittsburgh (last updated 2013-08-30):
  • Pittsburgh Show Offs
    Meeting Thursdays 6-8pm together with the CMU juggling club on the CMU campus (see below).
    website, facebook
  • Carnegie Mellon Masters of Flying Objects
    Meeting Sundays 4-6pm and Thursdays 6-8pm on or near the CMU campus. In nice summer weather, meetings are typically outside on the cut in front of Purnell; in the winter and in bad weather the Sunday meeting is the atrium of Newell Simon Hall and the Thursday meeting is in a changing location on campus. Growing group of students and nonstudents. See news section on the website or join mailing list for room announcements and short-term updates.
    website, mailing list, map
  • The Steel City Clown Brigade
    Clown troup with lots of children who do also some juggling. Meeting in Homestead, PA. See website for meeting times.
    website
  • Leave It To Beaver Valley Jugglers
    Jugglers meeting about 45min outside Pittsburgh in Baden, PA (Villa St. Joseph, Heritage Hall). Small group meeting regularly Sundays 3-5:30pm. Organizers of the annual Not Quite Pittsburgh Juggling Festival.
    website, map

If you know other regular meetings, let me know about it (mail: kaestner (at) cmu.edu). I don't run any of the clubs, I just wanted to make them easier to find. If you're looking for a performer, please email someone else, perhaps a club organizer. Inspired by Iain Murray's site.
